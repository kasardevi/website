Our Story

Kasar Devi Almond Oil is inspired by the serene temple village in Uttarakhand, where purity and nature blend harmoniously. We source the finest **Kashmiri Mamra almonds** directly from farmers and cold-press them with care in our **small-batch processing unit**.

Handpicked Kashmiri Mamra Almonds

Sourced directly from the valley, ensuring purity and authenticity.

Why It Matters?

Mamra almonds are **richer in oil content** and have **higher nutrition levels** compared to other varieties.

Cold-Pressed Perfection

Our traditional extraction process retains all nutrients.

Traditional Cold-Pressing

Unlike refined oils, we use a **cold-press expeller**, where almonds are **pressed at low temperatures**, ensuring **zero nutrient loss**.
