My Journey

A Path of Discovery, Passion & Craftsmanship

A Seed of Curiosity

My journey started in 2019 when I volunteered at Varanashi Organic Farms, near Mangalore, Karnataka. I spent my days working alongside farmers, understanding the essence of organic agriculture, and experiencing firsthand how nature and wellness are deeply intertwined. Little did I know, this experience would plant the seed for what I do today.

A Deep Dive into Wellness

After my travels, I found myself exploring the world of wellness more deeply. My bathroom shelf was soon filled with herbs, powders, and oils as I experimented with self-care formulations. I became obsessed with sourcing the finest herbs and understanding their properties—an obsession that naturally led me back to the farmers.

From Experimentation to Creation

I started small-batch processing of herbs like Amla and Moringa in my home setup. Drying, grinding, and packaging them into pure wellness products—it was an exciting phase. But soon, I realized that creating a truly unique and meaningful brand required focus and refinement.

A Shift in Focus

As I ventured into natural cosmetics and Ayurvedic oils, I discovered that regulatory challenges made large-scale production difficult. But I also found clarity—what if I dedicated myself fully to one product, perfected to its finest form? That's when I turned my attention to almond oil.

I set up my own small-scale processing unit at home, ensuring purity, hygiene, and a handcrafted touch. Every step, from sourcing almonds in Kashmir to pressing the oil, became a process I deeply enjoy. Kasar Devi Almond Oil was born—not just as a product, but as a philosophy of purity and craftsmanship.
