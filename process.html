From Farm to Bottle: A Pure Journey

At Kasar Devi, we follow a meticulous **cold-pressed extraction** method, ensuring that our almond oil retains all of its natural goodness.

1. Sourcing the Finest Kashmiri Mamra Almonds

We source handpicked **organic Kashmiri Mamra almonds** directly from trusted farmers. No middlemen, no compromises—just the best quality.

Why It Matters?

Mamra almonds are **richer in oil content** and have **higher nutrition levels** compared to other varieties.

Why Cold-Pressing?

Cold-pressing **preserves nutrients** by avoiding heat, making the oil **100% pure and nutrient-rich**.

2. Traditional Cold-Pressed Extraction

Unlike commercial refined oils, we use a **cold-press expeller**, where almonds are **pressed at low temperatures**, ensuring **zero nutrient loss**.

3. Naturally Settled, Not Refined

Instead of industrial refining, our oil is **naturally settled for 5-7 days**, allowing it to retain its **original texture, color, and aroma**.

No Chemicals, No Additives

We keep it 100% **pure and unfiltered**, ensuring that every drop is packed with natural goodness.

Handcrafted at Home

Our oil is processed in **small batches** in a controlled environment, ensuring top-notch hygiene and purity.

4. Bottled with Care

Finally, the oil is **hand-poured into glass bottles**, ensuring it reaches you **fresh and untouched by mass production**.

Experience the Difference

A small-batch, handcrafted process ensures that every bottle of **Kasar Devi Almond Oil** is as pure as nature intended.
