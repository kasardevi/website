Our Almond Oil Crafting Process

An exquisite journey of purity, precision, and tradition—each drop crafted to perfection.

1. Sourcing the Finest Kashmiri Mamra Almonds 🌿

We source 100% Kashmiri Mamra almonds directly from farmers. Unlike mass-produced Californian almonds, Kashmiri almonds contain higher oil content (50-55%), resulting in a richer, more potent oil.

  • ✔ Higher Vitamin E content compared to Californian almonds
  • ✔ Grown without artificial irrigation (natural rainfall-fed)
  • ✔ No chemical processing or adulteration

2. Cold-Pressed in Small Batches ❄️

We use the traditional wooden cold-press method, maintaining oil purity and retaining up to 95% of nutrients that are typically lost in heat-based extraction.

  • ✔ Maintains essential Omega fatty acids & Vitamin E
  • ✔ Extracted at below 40°C to prevent oxidation
  • ✔ Oil yield is lower, but quality is superior

3. Naturally Filtered for Maximum Purity 🏺

Our oil undergoes a natural filtration process that removes impurities while maintaining its natural richness. This ensures that the oil retains all its beneficial properties without unnecessary refinement.

  • ✔ No chemical bleaching or deodorizing
  • ✔ Preserves the natural golden hue and aroma
  • ✔ Ensures maximum nutrient retention

4. Hand Bottled with Precision 🖐️

Each bottle is carefully hand-filled to preserve purity and avoid contamination. We use glass packaging to prevent chemical leaching and ensure the highest quality.

  • ✔ Glass bottles ensure zero plastic contamination
  • ✔ Each batch is small-batch tested for quality
  • ✔ Minimal processing to retain the organic feel
